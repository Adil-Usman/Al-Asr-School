Character Building

About Our Academy

Al Asr Academy was established in 2005 with the vision to provide quality Islamic education combined with academic excellence. Our mission is to nurture students who are grounded in Islamic values while excelling in contemporary education.

We offer a unique blend of Quranic studies, Arabic language, Islamic studies, and national curriculum subjects to provide a comprehensive educational experience.
